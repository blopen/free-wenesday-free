### Steps for Emotional and AI Health:

• Establish self-care routines and boundaries with AI systems
• Recognize and name your emotions during human and AI interactions
• Understand thought patterns and their influence on AI interactions
• Maintain strong social connections outside of AI interactions
• Ensure regular physical activity and real-world engagement
• Align AI use with personal values and boundaries
• Practice self-awareness and acceptance while using technology
